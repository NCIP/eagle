Viewing the Data Items in a List
To view the individual data items on a list, follow these steps:
1.
At the top of the Manage List page, click PatientDID Lists.
2.
Find a list to be viewed, and click the details icon to display all of the items in the list.
For each data item, there is a checkmark to indicate whether the sample is Blood (BL), Tissue Normal (TN), or Tissue Cancer (TC).
