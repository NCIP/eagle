About EAGLE
Environmental And Genetic Lung cancer Etiology (EAGLE) is an interdisciplinary multi-center case-control study of lung cancer conducted in Milan, Italy. EAGLE enables investigators to explore the genetic determinants both of lung cancer and smoking. EAGLE analyzes genetic factors that interact with smoking in the etiology of the disease and genetic determinant identification of therapy efficacy and survival in the lung cancer subjects.
