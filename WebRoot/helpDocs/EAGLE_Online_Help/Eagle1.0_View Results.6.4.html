Resorting Column Results
The report column title that appears in bold with an arrow is the currently sorted column. To sort a column in a report, follow these steps:
1.
Click a column title in the report.
The column title is bold, and the column is sorted in ascending order.
2.
To sort the column in descending order, click the column title again.
The column is sorted in descending order.
For more information, see Class Comparison Report.
